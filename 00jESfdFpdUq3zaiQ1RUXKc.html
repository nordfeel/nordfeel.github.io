7 daily habits that extend your life

7 daily habits that extend your life

Despite everything that the ads try to tell us, the truth is that there is no magic potion to win the war on aging. The best way to deal with the signs of aging - prevent chronic diseases such as cardiovascular diseases( CVD), cancer and diabetes (DM), which accelerate the aging process and are the main causes of premature death in the modern world. Although population aging is a global trend, it is a complex process that can be slowed down with 7 simple everyday habits.

1. Variety of fruits and vegetables.

People who regularly eat a lot of different fruits and vegetables are less likely to suffer from chronic diseases. Such people maintain a healthy body weight and, according to statistics, live longer. Colorful fruits and vegetables are different nutrients and antioxidants. Their ingredients are included in the fight against aging in different ways. Anthocyanins, for example, are antioxidants that give blueberries a dark blue color and can limit the growth of cancer cells. Beta-carotene is a precursor of vitamin A, which gives carrots an orange color and promotes optimal immune function.

Nutrition experts recommend trying new fruits or vegetables every time we shop, and adding fruits and vegetables to every meal.

2. long-Chain omega-3 fatty acids (O-3 LC).

Long chain O-3 LC, eicosapentaenoic acid acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA), have unsurpassed anti-aging properties.

A study of the effect of EPA and DHA on chromosome aging shows that people with the highest blood levels of EPA and DHA belong to the group with the lowest incidence of CVD and the lowest rate of aging chromosomes'.

EPA and DHA are found in oily fish - salmon, tuna, flounder, mackerel, and sardines. If you are not a fan of fish and seafood or do not want to eat it every day, a suitable solution is to take fish oil supplements. This is a convenient, fast and no less effective way to get useful fatty acids acids.

3. Use dental floss every day.

Dental floss is not only good for gum health, but also promotes longevity. It removes the bacteria that cause periodontitis and lead to caries. These same bacteria can cause other health problems. If you do not observe proper hygiene in the oral cavity, bacteria around the teeth and gums will accumulate and enter the bloodstream. This, in turn, can cause inflammation and increase the risk of CVD.

4. Drink lots of green tea.

Green tea is not a magic potion, but it has many useful properties. This will help you get rid of several of them extra pounds that accumulate over the years.

Green tea contains an antioxidant called epigallocatechin-3-gallate, which when combined with caffeine can effectively help reduce body weight in just a few weeks.

5. Move more.

Lack of physical activity and weight loss muscle mass accelerates the aging process. Changes in quantity and quality that occur in muscles at the cellular level with age may be limited to active movement.

Early morning walking is a great way to improve your overall mobility. Cleaning your dacha in spring and summer is also a great way to move around. Squats, walking, bending, lifting - these are just some of the movements that are performed during several hours of work in the yard. A walk with your pet in the surrounding area is also available.

6. Forget about cigarettes.

It is no secret that Smoking is harmful to your health. But you seem to forget the harms of cigarettes damage your appearance. Studies show that Smoking not only increases the risk of CVD and lung diseases, but also activates enzymes that disrupt the elasticity of the skin. The result is frequent fine lines and gray skin.

7. Give up harsh diets.

Who doesn't want to get rid of it in the blink of an eye? extra pounds, especially on the eve of a holiday or special occasion? Quick results are tempting, but a strict diet is never a good idea. This is not a well-thought-out solution. Moreover, it can become a potential health hazard. Studies show that a strict diet can make you older, because the levels of energy and vigor decrease. In addition, restrictions can cause mental irritation. Sudden weight loss provokes the appearance of wrinkles and skin sagging, since the body has not had time to adapt to weight loss, especially if the skin has lost some of its elasticity over the years. Weight control is important for health, but acceptable limits are a maximum of 1-2 kilograms per week.
